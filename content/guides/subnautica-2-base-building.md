### Why Build a Base?

Your Lifepod is a cramped metal box. A real base gives you: full crafting, organized storage, vehicle docking, safety from predators, and unlimited food and power. Here's where to build and how to power it.

---

### Best Base Locations, Ranked

### Coral Gardens Early-Game Spots

| Location | Depth | Rating | Why |
|----------|-------|--------|-----|
| **Shallows (near Lifepod)** | 10-15m | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Safest, abundant Titanium/Copper/Quartz. Perfect for your first base. |
| **Coral/Anemone Fields (NW of Lifepod, 100-330m)** | 10-30m | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Resource-rich, very safe, solar + hydroelectric viable. The best balanced early spot. |
| **Camp One (Jelly Barrens)** | 20-50m | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Open space, food/water nearby, water currents for turbines. Good second base. |
| **Near Tufa Towers (E/SE 180m)** | 20-40m | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Near wrecks for blueprints, copper cave nearby, water currents. |

### Power Systems Compared

| Power Source | Best For | Materials | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Solar Panel** | Shallow bases (<50m) | 2× Quartz, 1× Copper | Free, infinite. Useless at night/depth. Build 3-4. |
| **Hydroelectric Turbine** | Bases near water currents | 2× Titanium Ingot, 1× Copper Wire, 1× Glass | Needs Power Transmitters. Best for mid-depth bases. |
| **Bioreactor** | Any depth | 2× Titanium Ingot, 2× Copper Ingot | Needs constant fuel (plants/fish). Good backup. |
| **Thermal Plant** | Hot zones (Graveyard, Hot Caves) | 3× Titanium, 2× Gold, 1× Sulfur | Infinite, stable. The best power source, but location-locked. |

**Rule of thumb:** Solar + Bioreactor for shallow bases. Thermal for hot biomes. Hydroelectric is great if you happen to build near a current.

### Base Building Order

1. **Room** (5× Titanium), Your first enclosed space.
2. **Hatch** (1× Titanium, 1× Quartz), Entry/exit point.
3. **Solar Panel(s)** (2× Quartz, 1× Copper each), Power.
4. **Fabricator** (1× Titanium, 1× Copper, 1× Quartz), Full crafting access.
5. **Wall Lockers**, Storage. Build at least 4.
6. **Battery Terminal** (2× Titanium, 2× Quartz, 1× Copper Wire), Recharge batteries.
7. **Moonpool** (5× Titanium), For Tadpole docking (mid-game).

### Pro Base Building Tips

- **Hub & Spoke model:** One main base + 2-3 small forward outposts (1 room, 1 Moonpool, 1 charger, 1 locker).
- **Structural integrity:** Keep at least 10 points above zero. Glass windows and hatches cost integrity.
- **Never rely on one power source.** Solar + Bioreactor is the safest combo for early bases.
- **Floor lockers hold 30 items**, place them sideways against walls for maximum density.
- **Grow food in your base.** Plant edible flora in growbeds so you never have to hunt for meals.
- **Build near resource clusters.** You'll be making dozens of supply runs, minimize travel time.
