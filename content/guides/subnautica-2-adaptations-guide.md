## What Are Angel Combs?

Angel Combs are glowing purple plants scattered across Proteus. Interacting with them unlocks **Adaptations**, permanent character upgrades that give you new abilities. They are the core progression system in Subnautica 2, gating access to biomes, resources, and story content.

---

## All Adaptations, in Order

### 1. Digestion (Must Get First)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Northeast of Lifepod, ~160m |
| **Depth** | Shallow (15-30m) |
| **Prerequisite** | None |

**What it does:** Lets you safely eat local fish and plants. Without Digestion, consuming alien food causes sickness and health loss. This is the #1 early-game mistake, new players starve because they didn't get this first.

**How to find:** From your Lifepod, swim northeast. Look for a glowing purple plant on the seabed. Interact with it.

---

### 2. Endurance (More Inventory)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Multiple: Sophie's Retreat + Cicada Wreck + Unauthorized NOA Mod |
| **Depth** | 30-80m |
| **Prerequisite** | Digestion |

**What it does:** Adds extra inventory slots and quickbar slots. More carrying capacity = fewer trips back to base.

---

### 3. Dexterity (Better Tool Handling)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Nahema's Lab, Hydrothermal Vents |
| **Depth** | 50-150m |
| **Prerequisite** | Digestion, Sonic Resonator |

**What it does:** Improves tool handling speed and efficiency.

**Note:** The Hydrothermal Vents are hot, you'll take damage without Heat Tolerance. Get in, interact with the Angel Comb, and get out quickly if you don't have Heat Tolerance yet.

---

### 4. Heat Tolerance (Access Hot Biomes)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Blighted Area Angel Comb |
| **Depth** | 20-40m |
| **Prerequisite** | Sonic Resonator (to clear Bloom Cankers blocking the area) |

**What it does:** Lets you survive in hot biomes, Hydrothermal Vents, Hot Caves, and certain deep Karakorum areas. Without it, hot water damages you rapidly.

**How to find:** The Blighted Area is east of the Old Habitat. You'll need the Sonic Resonator to clear Bloated Bloom Cankers blocking your path.

---

### 5. Axum Vision (Late Game, Story Critical)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Karakorum Power Plant |
| **Depth** | 200m+ |
| **Prerequisite** | Heat Tolerance, Tadpole with Depth Module, access to Karakorum |

**What it does:** Required to repair the Power Plant and progress the main story. This is the last adaptation in the current Early Access build.

---

## Progression Gate Summary

```
Digestion → Endurance → Dexterity → Heat Tolerance → Axum Vision
 │ │ │ │ │
 │ │ │ │ └── Late-game story
 │ │ │ └── Unlocks hot biomes (Gold, Sulfur, Lithium)
 │ │ └── Better tool efficiency
 │ └── More inventory = faster resource gathering
 └── Required to eat food = basic survival
```

---

## Frequently Missed Adaptations

- **Digestion can be missed entirely** if you don't explore northeast. You'll wonder why you keep getting sick from eating fish.
- **Heat Tolerance requires the Sonic Resonator.** You can't clear the Bloom Cankers blocking the Blighted Area without it. Get the Sonic Resonator from Old Habitat fragments first.
- **Dexterity is in a hot zone.** You'll need to tank some heat damage if you go for it before Heat Tolerance. Bring medkits.
