### The four Legendary Ships

| Ship | Location | Difficulty |
|------|----------|:---:|
| HMS Prince | SE corner (Fort Navassa) | ⭐⭐ |
| La Dama Negra | SW corner (Fort Serranilla) | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| El Impoluto | NW corner (Dry Tortuga) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| HMS Fearless + Royal Sovereign | NE corner (Fort Eleuthera) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

---

### HMS Prince, the warm-up
The easiest Legendary Ship. It fires mines when retreating. Avoid them. Circle behind it and unload broadsides into its rear. Standard tactics work here.

---

### La Dama Negra, hit the rear
This ship is fast but its weak point is the stern. Circle it constantly. Stay behind it. Fire mortars when it tries to create distance. Do not chase it head-on.

---

### El Impoluto, the rammer
This ship rams you. Repeatedly. Brace for impact when it charges. Fire mortars while it circles back for another run. Use heavy shot to slow it down between ramming attempts.

---

### HMS Fearless + Royal Sovereign, the twin fight
Two Legendary Ships at once. The hardest naval encounter in the game. Focus one at a time, but try to finish both around the same time. If you kill one and take too long on the second, reinforcements may arrive.

Strategy: disable Fearless first (it is faster). Use fire barrels and swivel guns. When it is crippled, switch to Royal Sovereign. Finish Fearless, then clean up.

---

### Fire barrel trick

Fire barrels deal massive damage to Legendary Ships. More importantly, Legendary Ships often fire their own fire barrels at you. Shoot those barrels with your swivel gun before they reach you. The explosion damages the enemy ship that fired them. Three hits disables most ships.
