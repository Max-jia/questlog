### Why hunt

Skins upgrade your health, ammo pouches, and holsters. Buying materials costs reales. Hunting is free. Every island has animals. Kill them. Skin them. Upgrade.

---

### Land animals

| Animal | Location | Drops |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Deer | Forests, grasslands | Deer Hide |
| Jaguar | Jungle islands | Jaguar Pelt |
| Tapir | Coastal areas | Tapir Hide |
| Boar | Forested islands | Boar Hide |

---

### Marine animals

| Animal | Location | Drops |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Shark | Open ocean | Shark Skin |
| Whale | Deep ocean | Whale Blubber |
| Stingray | Shallow reefs | Stingray Hide |

Harpoon sharks from the Jackdaw. It is faster and safer than swimming.

---

### Legendary animals

Each legendary animal drops a unique skin. These upgrade specific equipment slots. Marked on your map after you discover the corresponding island. Hunt them as soon as you can reach them.
