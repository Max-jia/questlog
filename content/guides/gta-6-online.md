## Is GTA 6 Online at Launch?

**Short answer: Probably not on day one.** Rockstar has described GTA 6 as a single-player experience at launch. GTA V followed the same pattern, story mode launched September 17, 2013. GTA Online launched two weeks later on October 1.

Expect a similar window: story mode first, online mode within weeks to a month.

---

### What Will GTA Online 2.0 Look Like?

### GTA V Online's Legacy

GTA V Online launched with 16 players per session. It grew over 10+ years into one of the most profitable entertainment products in history, generating over $8 billion in revenue. It now supports 32 players, has hundreds of vehicles, dozens of heists, businesses, properties, and a full economy.

GTA 6 Online will build on this foundation from day one.

### Will My GTA V Online Progress Transfer?

**Almost no.** GTA 6 is a new game with a new city, new engine iteration, and a new economy. Rockstar treats each GTA as a fresh start for online progression. Your cars, properties, and money from GTA V Online will stay in Los Santos.

---

### What Happens to GTA V Online?

GTA V Online will not shut down. Rockstar has invested too much in it and it still generates massive revenue. The likely scenario:
- GTA V Online continues running with reduced updates
- GTA 6 Online becomes the primary focus for new content
- Both coexist, similar to how Rockstar maintained GTA Online while developing GTA 6

---

### When Will We Know More?

Rockstar has been completely silent about GTA 6 Online. Trailers 1 and 2 focused exclusively on story mode. A dedicated Online trailer is expected closer to launch, likely October or November 2026, or even post-launch in December.
