## The Protagonists, Rumored Actors

Rockstar has not officially confirmed GTA 6's voice cast. Historically, Rockstar keeps voice actors secret until after launch, GTA V's cast wasn't confirmed until players datamined the credits.

| Character | Rumored Actor | Known For | Status |
|-----------|--------------|-----------|--------|
| **Jason Duval** | Dylan Rourke | TV and indie film roles | Unconfirmed |
| **Lucia Caminos** | Manni L. Perez | TV appearances, theater | Unconfirmed |

The strongest evidence for Manni L. Perez as Lucia comes from voice analysis of Trailer 1, fans have matched her voice pattern to previous acting reels. But until Rockstar confirms it, treat all casting as unverified.

---

### Why Rockstar Keeps Voice Actors Secret

Rockstar's policy of not revealing voice actors before launch is deliberate. Unlike movies or TV shows where casting is marketing, Rockstar wants players to see the characters, not the actors. They've maintained this policy since GTA III in 2001, with three exceptions: Ray Liotta (Tommy Vercetti, GTA: Vice City), Samuel L. Jackson (Officer Tenpenny, GTA: San Andreas), and Peter Fonda (The Truth, GTA: San Andreas).
