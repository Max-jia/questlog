## Vehicle Customization, What's New

### Interior Customization (New to the Series!)
For the first time in GTA, you can customize your vehicle's interior, seats, dashboard, steering wheel. This was confirmed through the Ultimate Edition product descriptions.

### Off-Road Conversion Kits
Regular vehicles can be converted to off-road specs at specific mod shops. The **Ganado Retro Build** is a pre-assembled kit for Jason's pickup truck.

### Exclusive Mod Shops
| Shop | Specialization | Access |
|------|---------------|--------|
| **Rideout Customs** | Styling, donk builds, street mods | Ultimate Edition |
| **One-Eyed Willie's** | Off-road mods, hand-painted art, Mud Club builds | Ultimate Edition |

### Classic Car Restoration (New Activity)
A "Special Commission" side quest chain from collector Wyman. Find abandoned classic cars across Leonida and restore them to their former glory, GTA's take on the *Forza Horizon* barn find system.

## The RDR2-Style Loadout System

GTA 6 adopts Red Dead Redemption 2's weapon management:

- **You can't carry 50 guns at once.** Choose your loadout before missions.
- **Weapon lockers** at garages and safehouses store your arsenal.
- Jason and Lucia have **separate loadouts**, plan tactically for missions where both are present.
- Larger rifles require a **sling** to carry on your back (seen in leaked 2022 footage).
