### Proteus Map, Two Major Regions

Subnautica 2 currently has two main regions in Early Access. You start in the safe Coral Gardens and eventually venture into the deep, hostile Karakorum.

---

### Coral Gardens (Starting Region, 0-150m)

Your home for the first 10-15 hours. Safe, abundant resources, gradually increasing danger.

### Shallows (0-20m)
**Your spawn biome.** Safe, bright, full of Titanium, Copper, and Quartz.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Titanium (abundant), Copper (cave ceilings), Quartz (seabed + caves), Acidic Raion Pouch |
| **Threats** | None. Safest biome in the game. |
| **Key locations** | Lifepod (spawn), starting resource nodes |

---

### Jelly Barrens (20-50m)
First exploration zone. Home to Camp One and the Digestion Angel Comb.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Lead, Water Slugs, Angel Comb (Digestion), Macaron Sponges |
| **Threats** | Hammerhead (low), Marrowbreach (medium) |
| **Key locations** | Camp One (second Habitat Builder fragment), NE Angel Comb |

---

### Old Habitat / Plateaus (20-60m)
Ruined alien settlements. Critical blueprint zone.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Blueprints (!), Necrolei Cysts, Silver, Habitat Builder fragments |
| **Threats** | Deepwing Brooder Leviathan (high, avoid or flee) |
| **Key locations** | Room blueprint, Processor blueprint, Sonic Resonator fragments |

---

### Blighted Area (20-40m)
**Blocked by Bloom Cankers until you have the Sonic Resonator.** Contains the Heat Tolerance Angel Comb.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Angel Comb (Heat Tolerance) |
| **Threats** | Bloated Bloom Cankers (environmental, cleared with Sonic Resonator) |
| **Access** | East of Old Habitat |

---

### Hydrothermal Vents / Graveyard (50-150m)
Hot, dangerous, resource-rich. **Requires Heat Tolerance.**

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Gold (exclusive), Sulfur (exclusive), Lithium |
| **Threats** | Houndgar (medium), Marrowbreach (medium), heat damage |
| **Key locations** | Nahema's Lab (Dexterity Angel Comb), Tailings Village |

---

### The Hole (100m+)
Deep crater. Home to the most feared creature in Coral Gardens.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Lithium, rare fragments |
| **Threats** | Great Jaw Leviathan (high, traps you in a clam-like mouth) |
| **Key locations** | Cicada Wreckage |

---

### Sea Anemone Forest (30-50m)
West of Old Habitat. Dense with flora.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Necrolei Cysts (abundant) |
| **Threats** | Low |
| **Key locations** | West of Old Habitat |

---

### Karakorum (Late-Game Region, 100-450m)

Accessed via a deep trench **east of Coral Gardens**. Guarded by a **Collector Leviathan** patrolling the chasm. You need the Tadpole with Depth Module to explore effectively.

### Observatory (140m)
The entry hub of Karakorum. Central, safe.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Celestine, Conduit Crystal |
| **Threats** | Needler Nest (medium), Needler Mango (medium) |
| **Key locations** | Axum Observatory, Rosetta Stone, Research Outpost, Modification Station blueprint |

---

### Power Plant (200m+)
Main story objective. Requires Axum Vision.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Celestine, Atacamite, Conduit Crystal |
| **Threats** | Epicurean (high), Surge Jelly (medium) |
| **Key locations** | Power Plant (story objective), Axum Vision Angel Comb |

---

### Metal Farms (300m+)
The deepest accessible zone. Endgame resources.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Resources** | Troilite (rare, limited spawns), Axum Bacterial Culture |
| **Threats** | Epicurean (high), second Collector Leviathan |
| **Key locations** | Alterra Starship Wreck, endgame farming zone |

---

### Creature Threat Levels

### Friendly / Passive
| Creature | Location | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Water Slugs | Coral Gardens seabed | Water source |
| Cradle Shootroots | Shallows | Harvestable flora |
| Curtain Gorgon | Shallows | Decorative |

### Hostile, Low Threat
| Creature | Location | How to Deal |
|----------|----------|------------|
| Hammerhead | Coral Gardens | Dodge. Low damage. |
| Nibbler Mango | Coral Gardens | Ignore. Minimal threat. |
| Mimic Pylon | Coral Gardens | Avoid. Annoying but not dangerous. |

### Hostile, Medium Threat
| Creature | Location | How to Deal |
|----------|----------|------------|
| Marrowbreach | Jelly Barrens, Vents | Sonic Resonator stuns. Keep distance. |
| Houndgar | Hydrothermal Vents | Fast, aggressive. Dodge and use Wakemaker. |
| Surge Jelly | Karakorum | Electric attacks. Keep distance. |
| Needler Mango | Karakorum Observatory | Projectile attacks. Strafe. |
| Twin Sitaray | Karakorum | Pair tactics. Separate and focus one. |

### Hostile, High Threat
| Creature | Location | How to Deal |
|----------|----------|------------|
| Deepwing Brooder Leviathan | Old Habitat | **Avoid.** Use Dash to escape. Do not engage. |
| Great Jaw Leviathan | The Hole | Traps you in clam mouth. Sonic Resonator to escape. |
| Epicurean | Karakorum Power Plant, Metal Farms | Heavy damage. Impact Armor helps. Keep moving. |

### Hostile, Extreme Threat
| Creature | Location | How to Deal |
|----------|----------|------------|
| Collector Leviathan | Trench (Coral→Karakorum), Metal Farms | **Do not engage.** Patrols predictably. Wait for it to pass, then dash through. Cavitation Muffler reduces detection range. |

### Survival Tips by Biome

- **Shallows:** Safe. Farm resources freely.
- **Jelly Barrens:** Stay near the seabed. Hammerheads roam mid-water.
- **Old Habitat:** Approach carefully. Check for Deepwing before entering buildings.
- **Hydrothermal Vents:** Bring medkits if you don't have Heat Tolerance. Get in and out.
- **The Hole:** Hug the walls. The Great Jaw patrols the center.
- **Karakorum:** Always have a fully charged Tadpole. Carry a Repair Tool. Save before entering the trench.
