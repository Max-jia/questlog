### Build the Moonpool before the Tadpole

You cannot charge the Tadpole without a Moonpool. Building the Tadpole first means you have a dead vehicle sitting on the ocean floor. Build the Moonpool first. Then the Vehicle Fabricator inside it. Then the Tadpole.

Materials: 5× Titanium. That is it. The cheapest major base module.

---

### Vehicle Fabricator

Build this inside the Moonpool. Materials: 2× Titanium Ingot, 1× Copper Ingot, 2× Glass. This is where you craft the Tadpole and swap chassis.

---

### Tadpole Dock

Optional but recommended. 2× Titanium Ingot, 1× Silver Ingot, 2× Copper Wire. Place it next to the Moonpool. It auto-charges and repairs the Tadpole when docked. Faster than manual repair.

---

### Placement tips

Build the Moonpool on the side of your base facing open water. The Tadpole needs a clear path to enter and exit. Do not build it facing a cliff or your other base modules.
