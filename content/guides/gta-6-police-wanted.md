## The GTA V Wanted System, Why It Needs an Overhaul

GTA V's police system, while iconic, had flaws cops would materialize out of thin air in remote areas. Helicopters spawned instantly. Escaping a 5-star wanted level felt more like exploiting game mechanics than clever evasion.

GTA 6, built on the RDR2 engine iteration, has the potential for a dramatically more realistic system.

---

### RDR2's Witness System, Likely Coming to GTA 6

RDR2 introduced a witness mechanic: commit a crime, and nearby NPCs become witnesses who will run to report you. You can stop them, intimidate, bribe, or silence them. If they reach law enforcement, you get a wanted level.

**Leaked 2022 GTA 6 footage** showed NPC behavior consistent with this system. If it makes it into the final game, GTA 6's police interactions will feel far more organic than GTA V's "shoot one person → instant 1 star."

---

### What Might Change With the Wanted System

| Feature | GTA V | GTA VI (Expected) |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| **Witnesses** | ❌ No witness system | ✅ NPCs witness crimes and report them |
| **Police spawn** | Instant, anywhere | Gradual response, realistic deployment |
| **Escape** | Hide in alley for 30 seconds | Evade search perimeter. Police use patterns, not timers |
| **Helicopters** | Spawn immediately at 3+ stars | Delayed, must deploy from nearest airbase |
| **Surveillance** | ❌ None | Possible cameras, ANPR (license plate readers) in modern Vice City |
| **Non-lethal arrests** | ❌ Kill on sight at high stars | Possible, police behavior based on crime severity |
| **Undercover units** | ❌ None | Possible for drug/organized crime missions |

---

### Modern Police Technology in Vice City

Set in 2026, GTA 6's Vice City has modern policing tools that the 2013 Los Santos didn't:

- **Drones**, Police surveillance drones are now widespread. Could be used to track you from above.
- **Body cameras**, NPC cops might wear body cams, meaning crimes are automatically reported.
- **License plate readers**, Stolen cars may be automatically flagged.
- **Smartphone tracking**, If your in-game phone is tracked, hiding becomes harder.

---

### How It Affects Gameplay

If these systems make it into GTA 6, the gameplay loop changes significantly:

- **Planning matters more.** You'll need to scout locations, identify witnesses, and plan escape routes before committing crimes.
- **Silencers become valuable.** A silenced weapon might let you eliminate a witness without alerting others.
- **Vehicle switching becomes strategic.** Stolen vehicles may be tracked. You'll want your own "clean" cars.
- **Safehouses matter.** Having a place to lay low while heat dies down becomes more important.
