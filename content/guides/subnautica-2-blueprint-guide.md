### Most Commonly Missed Blueprints

**Processor.** Many players build a base without ever finding this. It's in Old Habitat and lets you make ingots with 40% fewer materials. Without it, you're wasting Titanium on every craft.

**Growbed.** Found at Karakorum Observatory (late-game). Without it, you can't farm Lucifer Rotsac for rubber, meaning constant trips to the surface. Get this ASAP once you reach Karakorum.

**Modification Station.** Unlocks the Bioscanner and Feedback Resonator. If you're exploring Karakorum without these upgrades, you're making things much harder than they need to be.
