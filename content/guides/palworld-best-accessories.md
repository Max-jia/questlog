You have limited accessory slots. Most accessories are situational. A few are mandatory. Here is what to wear and when to swap.

---

### The three you never take off

| Accessory | Effect | Priority |
|-----------|--------|:---:|
| Attack Pendant | Increases damage output | 1 |
| Defense Pendant | Reduces damage taken | 2 |
| Giga Shield | Absorbs hundreds of damage before your HP drops | 3 |

These three should be on your character at all times. Do not experiment. Do not swap them out for curiosity. Attack, defense, shield. Everything else is situational.

---

### Situational swaps

| Accessory | When to Wear |
|-----------|-------------|
| Heat Undershirt | Volcano biome. Prevents heat damage. |
| Cold Undershirt | Northern snow biome. Required for Frostallion hunting. |
| Ring of Mercy | Wild Pal catching. Prevents accidental kills. Take it off for boss fights. |
| Life Pendant | Early game if you keep dying. Replace with Defense Pendant once you have better armor. |

---

### Accessories to skip

- Work Speed pendant. Your Pals do the work. You do not need this.
- Stamina pendant. Food buffs give you enough stamina.
- Anything with "minor" in the name. The percentage boost is too small to matter.

---

### Where to get the good ones

Attack and Defense Pendants drop from dungeon chests and high-level Pals. The Giga Shield is crafted at level 28. The undershirts are crafted from cloth and elemental organs.

Do not buy accessories from merchants. They are overpriced. Farm dungeons.
