### Who Gets to Play First?

New Zealand players start at 11:00 AM UTC on November 18, a full 13 hours before US East Coast players. This has led to the infamous **"New Zealand trick"** on Xbox:

1. Go to Xbox Settings → System → Language & Location
2. Change Location to **New Zealand**
3. Restart console
4. Game unlocks at NZ midnight (which is 6:00 AM EST / 3:00 AM PST on Nov 18)

This trick has worked for nearly every major Xbox release. PlayStation players cannot do this, PSN accounts are region-locked to their store.
