Naval combat is half the game. Most players just spam broadsides and wonder why they lose to bigger ships. Here is how to actually win.

---

### When to use each weapon

| Weapon | Range | Best For |
|--------|:---:|-------|
| Broadside cannons | Close | Main damage. Use these most. |
| Mortars | Long | Soften targets before closing distance. |
| Fire barrels | Any | Massive damage. Shoot enemy barrels with swivel guns. |
| Ram | Close | Finish off crippled ships. Only with hull upgrade. |
| Swivel guns | Any | Shoot weak points on enemy ships. Inaccurate at long range. |

---

### Board, do not sink

Sinking gives you half the loot. Boarding gives you full resources, crew replenishment, free repairs, and the option to add the ship to Kenway's Fleet for passive income. Board every ship you can.

---

### Fire barrel trick

Fire barrels deal the most damage in naval combat. But the real trick: enemy ships fire their own fire barrels at you. Shoot those barrels with your swivel gun before they hit you. The explosion damages the enemy ship. Three hits disables most ships.

---

### Legendary Ship prep

Before attempting any Legendary Ship:
- Elite hull
- Elite cannons
- Elite mortars
- All three officers (Tobias Smith adds an extra broadside volley)
