### Why GTA Soundtracks Matter

GTA V's soundtrack cost Rockstar an estimated **$50M+ in licensing fees** and featured 240+ songs. The radio is a core part of the GTA experience, players spend hours driving and listening. GTA VI, set in modern Miami, will likely feature a mix of:

- 80s throwbacks (Vice City's legacy)
- Modern hip-hop and Latin music
- Electronic/dance (Miami club scene)
- Haitian and Caribbean influences
- Satirical talk radio and news
