### What else to expect

GTA V launched with 300+ vehicles. GTA 6 will likely match or exceed that. The coastal setting means boats will be more important than in any previous GTA. Aircraft are confirmed through the functional airport.

Vehicle interior customization is new to the series. You can modify seats, dashboard, and steering wheel.
