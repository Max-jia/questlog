### Where the new Pals actually spawn

The highest concentration of new Pals is in two zones:

**Sunreach (floating islands):** Faleris, Orserk, sky-region variants, Vanusa, Dualith. Accessible via flying mount or teleporter after reaching the altitude threshold.

**World Tree:** Wispaw, Venusa, Aegidron, Shaolong, Dandilord, Hoodle, and most night-spawn variants. Endgame zone. Come prepared.

**New small islands:** Various elemental variants scattered across the seven new small islands added in 1.0. Volcano, desert, and ruin biomes each have unique spawns.

---

### Which new Pals to prioritize

Catch these first:
- Dupin (early game, easy find, fun)
- Soul Mora (fast water mount, replaces earlier water Pals)
- Seek (ground type, pairs well with Anubis for coverage)
- Dualith (endgame tank, worth the cave dive)
- Vanusa (CC machine, good for raid waves)
