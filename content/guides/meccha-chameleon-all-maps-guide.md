## Meccha Chameleon Maps Overview

There are **7 official maps**, each with its own hiding meta. Plus hundreds more on the Steam Workshop. Here's every official map, ranked easiest to hardest for hiders:

| Map | Difficulty for Hiders | Best Strategy |
|-----|---------------------|---------------|
| Penguin Hotel | ⭐ Easy | Dense clutter, many rooms, lots of vertical options |
| Mansion | ⭐ Easy | Maximum visual noise, classic map everyone knows |
| Sewer | ⭐⭐ Medium | Dark lighting helps, but limited cover |
| Sugar Land | ⭐⭐ Medium | Bright colors are easy to match, but open areas |
| Backrooms | ⭐⭐⭐ Hard | Sparse decor, liminal spaces, camouflage must be perfect |
| Indoor Country | ⭐⭐⭐ Hard | Bright and open, few hiding nooks |
| Osaka | ⭐⭐⭐ Hard | Smallest map, tight spaces, very exposed |

## 1. Hide-and-Seek Mansion

**The classic.** A sprawling Victorian mansion packed with rooms, furniture, and decorations. The most cluttered map, perfect for creative hiders.

### Best Hiding Spots

| Spot | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **Library bookshelves** | Crouch between book spines. Match the wood grain and book colors. Paint darker stripes to simulate book edges. |
| **Ceiling pillars** | Climb to the ceiling and lie flat along a pillar. Most seekers never look up here. |
| **Horse statue** | Hide inside or on top of the statue. Paint as the horse, bronze/copper tones with metallic slider up. |
| **Kitchen wall** | Press against wall posters and replicate the design. Match the exact poster colors with the eyedropper. |
| **Bathroom tiles** | Crouch in a corner where walls meet. Match the tile pattern, use the grid to align your body stripes. |
| **Balloon banner** | Climb to the highest balloon on the back wall banner and match its color. |

### Seeker Tips for This Map
- Check behind the front door, it's the #1 noob trap.
- Scan under the pool table in the game room.
- The chandelier and ceiling moldings are popular vertical spots.

## 2. Indoor Country

A bright, farm-themed map with hay bales, pumpkins, animal cutouts, and a barn. Smaller than Mansion but more open.

### Best Hiding Spots

| Spot | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **Cow standees** | Perch on a cow cutout. Paint black and white patches, the patterns do the camouflaging for you. |
| **Hay bales** | Squeeze between stacked hay bales and the barn wall. Match the golden-brown straw texture. |
| **Pumpkin patch** | Crouch among the pumpkins. Paint yourself orange and use the curl-up pose to mimic a pumpkin's shape. |
| **Ceiling clouds** | Attach to the ceiling where painted clouds are. Match the cloud white/blue gradient. |
| **Green crates** | Tuck behind large green crates near the barn entrance. |

---

## 3. Sewer

Dark, industrial, and gritty. Low lighting means color matching is less critical, **texture and silhouette** matter more here.

### Best Hiding Spots

| Spot | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **Graffiti walls** | This is the best spot on the map. Paint yourself to match the graffiti scribbles and brick texture. Replicate patterns across your body, lines should match up with the wall art. |
| **Ceiling pipes** | Hide behind overhead pipes in the darkest sections. Use the shadow to your advantage, keep your own shadow OFF. |
| **Oil barrels** | Lie flat on top of a barrel. Match the rusted metal texture (high roughness, medium metallic). |
| **Side lockers** | Hide inside open lockers. Paint to match the dark interior. |

### Seeker Tips for This Map
- Use the shotgun muzzle flash to light up dark pipes.
- Graffiti walls are popular, check them carefully but don't spend all round there.

---

## 4. Backrooms

Liminal office space aesthetic. Sparse, repetitive, and unsettling. You have fewer objects to hide among, so **perfect camouflage is essential**.

### Best Hiding Spots

| Spot | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **Vending machines** | Climb to the top row of products. Match the dark shadow at the very top of the machine interior. |
| **Chair stacks** | Crouch underneath stacked chairs. The natural shadow there hides imperfect paint jobs. |
| **Exit signs** | Paint yourself as an exit sign on the wall. Use the exact red/green from the nearby sign with the eyedropper. |
| **Wall bikes** | Match the mounted bicycles on the wall in the main corridor. |
| **Birthday party room** | Hide among the cakes and balloons. Lots of colors to blend with. |

---

## 5. Penguin Hotel

The **best map for hiders**. Dense, multi-room, stuffed with penguin plushies, curtains, furniture, and decorations. Hunters have a nightmare here.

### Best Hiding Spots

| Spot | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **Stuffed penguin toys** | Sit beside penguin plushies. Match their black/white coloring and the exact pose. You'll look like another plushie. |
| **White curtains** | Paint yourself to match the curtains hanging between pillars. Go high, hunters rarely check above eye level. |
| **Ballroom rafters** | Hide in the rafters next to balloons in the grand ballroom. |
| **Under the beds** | In the bedroom section, slide under a bed and match the floor shadow. |
| **Second floor hallway** | Use the tables, statues, and large dice blocks for cover. |

---

## 6. Osaka

The **newest and smallest** map. A tight Japanese city street with shops, signs, trucks, and octopus decorations. exposed, you need clever positioning.

### Best Hiding Spots

| Spot | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **Truck bed / underneath** | Match the dark shadow under the truck carefully. Low to the ground spots are overlooked. |
| **Overhead signs** | Climb onto shop signs above street level. The large Octopus wall decoration is a popular but effective anchor. |
| **Open shops** | Blend into side walls, metal fans, or stacked crates inside the small shops. |
| **Trash piles** | Hide among trash bags and discarded items near the alley. |

---

## 7. Sugar Land

A bright, colorful candy-themed map. Lollipops, gingerbread houses, chocolate fountains, and gumdrop mountains. The bright palette makes color matching easy but visibility is high.

### Best Hiding Spots

| Spot | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| **Chocolate fountain** | Match the rich brown textures beside the flowing chocolate fountain. |
| **Candy houses** | Hide on top of or between gingerbread houses. Match the frosting and candy colors. |
| **Gumdrop piles** | Blend into the candy mountains. Pick one gumdrop color and match it exactly. |
| **Strawberries on the cake** | The white cake in the center has strawberries on top. Hide as one of them. |
| **Gingerbread men circle** | Crouch in the circle of gingerbread men statues and match the floor pattern. |

## Universal Hiding Rules for All Maps

1. **Hide where there's visual clutter.** Empty walls are death traps.
2. **Use height.** Most seekers scan at eye level. Ceilings, rafters, and high corners are blind spots.
3. **Break your silhouette with poses.** Your shape matters more than your color.
4. **Rotate spots every round.** Don't let veterans learn your patterns.
5. **Check all angles before the hunt phase.** Use free cam (4) to inspect your disguise from every direction.
