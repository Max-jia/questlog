### Vice City Nostalgia Is Everywhere

GTA 6 is set in modern Vice City, but Rockstar is weaving the original 1986 game's legacy throughout. Here's every callback, reference, and easter egg found so far.

---

### Confirmed References

### Vercetti Estate (Tommy's Mansion)
The Hawk & Little Morgan revolvers (Ultimate Edition bonus) are described as being sourced from the **Vercetti Estate.** This means Tommy Vercetti's mansion from GTA: Vice City (1986) canonically exists in the HD universe. Whether Tommy himself appears is unknown, but his legacy is part of the world.

### Phil Cassidy on TV
Phil Cassidy, the one-armed gun nut from GTA: Vice City and GTA III, appears on in-game television in GTA 6. This is significant because it confirms that characters from the 3D universe (1986 Vice City) exist in some form in the HD universe (2026 Vice City).

### Tommy's Hawaiian Shirt Pattern
The pre-order weapon skin has Tommy Vercetti's iconic blue Hawaiian shirt pattern. It's described as a "Vice City-inspired weapon pattern", a direct tribute to the protagonist of the original game.

### The '55 Vapid Stanier
The pre-order bonus vehicle is a yellow-and-white 1955 Vapid Stanier, a direct callback to the classic cars of 1980s Vice City. The color scheme is reminiscent of the taxis from the original game.
