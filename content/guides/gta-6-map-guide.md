### How Big Is the GTA 6 Map?

| Metric | GTA V | GTA VI |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Total land area | ~49 sq miles | ~83 sq miles (estimated) |
| Major cities | 1 (Los Santos) | 1 (Vice City) + 4 sub-cities |
| Enterable interiors | ~150 | **700+** |
| Drive circumference | ~15 min | ~35 min |
| Regions | 1 county | **6 distinct regions** |

The map is estimated 1.5-2× larger than GTA V, but density is the real story, nearly every area has meaningful content.

### The Six Regions of Leonida

### 1. Vice City (Urban Core)
**Inspiration:** Modern Miami

| Neighborhood | Vibe |
|---|---|
| **Ocean Beach / Vice Beach** | Art Deco hotels, white sand, tourist crowds |
| **South Beach** | Nightclubs, street racing after 10 PM |
| **Downtown** | Skyscrapers, corporate offices, high-end shopping |
| **Little Havana** | Cuban culture, Spanish-speaking NPCs, unique food |
| **Little Haiti** | Haitian Creole community, dense residential |
| **Stockyard** | Wynwood-style arts district, graffiti, races |
| **Belville** | Suburban sprawl, strip malls, Jason's mother's home |
| **Venetian Islands** | Luxury mansions, boat access, late-game properties |
| **Port Vice City** | Cruise ships, container docks, smuggling ops |
| **International Airport** | Fully functional, steal planes |

---

### 2. Leonida Keys
**Inspiration:** Florida Keys

A chain of tropical islands connected by long bridges. Expect boat-focused gameplay, diving spots, luxury resorts, and smuggling routes between islands. The Keys are where Jason worked for drug runners after the army.

---

### 3. Grassrivers
**Inspiration:** The Everglades

Vast swampland with airboats, alligators, dense wildlife, and hidden criminal operations. Mud tracks, swamp shacks, and airboat chases are shown in trailers. Expect off-road vehicles to be essential here.

---

### 4. Port Gellhorn
**Inspiration:** Panama City / Forgotten Gulf Coast

Industrial port town with shipping containers, docks, cheap motels, and biker gang territory. A working-class contrast to Vice City's glitz. Likely a hub for smuggling and trafficking missions.

---

### 5. Ambrosia
**Inspiration:** Rural Florida inland

Small towns, factories, rail yards, and open farmland. Home to biker gangs and blue-collar crime. The quieter, more desolate side of Leonida, perfect for hideouts and low-profile operations.

---

### 6. Mount Kalaga National Park
**Inspiration:** Florida panhandle highlands / Georgia border

Forested mountain region with off-road trails, hunting grounds, fishing spots, and scenic overlooks. The highest elevation in the game, expect sniping, wildlife encounters, and mountain climbing.

### Map Size Comparison, What 1.5-2× GTA V Really Means

GTA V's map felt large because much of it was empty mountain and desert. GTA VI's Leonida is not bigger, it's **denser**. With 700+ enterable interiors (vs ~150 in GTA V), you can enter buildings in nearly every neighborhood. The world is designed for depth, not sprawl.

**Community estimate:** Crossing the full map by car takes approximately 35 minutes. A straight-line drive across takes about 6 minutes.
