The magic system in Moonlight Peaks has two branches: spells (equipped and cast with a mana bar) and potions (crafted and consumed). You unlock both through the Witch Coven questline. Here is every recipe, every spell, and which ones are actually worth your ingredients.

---

## How to Unlock Magic

1. Reach 3 hearts with any witch NPC (Luna is the easiest; see the Romance Guide).
2. The NPC gives you a quest: "The Initiate's Trial." Deliver 10 Moonstones (mine levels 20-40) + 5 Glowflowers + 1 Crystal Ball (buy from the traveling merchant for 500G).
3. Complete the trial. You unlock the Spellbook (spell menu) and Potion Station (crafting table upgrade).
4. New potions and spells unlock as you raise friendship with individual witches. Each witch teaches different spells.

---

## Spell List

Spells consume mana. Your mana bar starts at 50 and expands to 200 with upgrades (buy from Luna's shop, 2,000G each level).

### Farming Spells

| Spell | Mana | Effect | Unlock |
|-------|:---:|--------|--------|
| Water Rune | 10 | Waters all crops in a 3×3 area | Automatic (tutorial spell) |
| Growth Surge | 30 | Instantly advances 1 crop to maturity | Luna, 5 hearts |
| Harvest Sweep | 20 | Harvests all ready crops in range | Morgana, 5 hearts |
| Pest Ward | 15 | Prevents bat raids for 3 nights | Luna, 7 hearts |

Water Rune and Growth Surge are game-changers. With Water Rune, you never touch the watering can again. With Growth Surge, you can force-harvest Ghost Peppers every other night instead of waiting 4 nights.

### Combat Spells

| Spell | Mana | Effect | Unlock |
|-------|:---:|--------|--------|
| Bat Swarm | 25 | Summons bats that deal 30 damage to all enemies | Corvus, 3 hearts |
| Shadow Bolt | 15 | Single-target projectile, 50 damage | Corvus, 5 hearts |
| Mist Form | 40 | Become invulnerable for 10 seconds | Morgana, 7 hearts |
| Moonbeam | 50 | AoE holy damage, 100 damage, stuns undead | Morgana, 10 hearts |

Shadow Bolt is your main combat spell. Bat Swarm for groups. Mist Form is the emergency button. Moonbeam is overkill for 95% of combat encounters; only needed for the deep mine boss (level 100+).

### Utility Spells

| Spell | Mana | Effect | Unlock |
|-------|:---:|--------|--------|
| Night Vision | 5 | See in the dark for 2 hours (real: 2 min) | Automatic |
| Charm Mortal | 30 | Humans become friendly for 30 seconds | Seraphina, 6 hearts |
| Shadow Step | 20 | Teleport 10 meters forward | Corvus, 8 hearts |
| Bat Form (Enhanced) | 0 | Bat form flight speed doubled | Luna, 10 hearts |

Shadow Step is incredible for mining; teleport past walls to reach ore veins without walking. Enhanced Bat Form makes cross-map travel nearly instant.

## Potion Recipes

### Energy & Health

| Potion | Ingredients | Effect |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Blood Flask | 1 Glass Vial + 3 Nightshade | Restore 50% energy |
| Enhanced Blood Flask | 1 Glass Vial + 3 Nightshade + 1 Moonstone | Restore 100% energy |
| Vampire's Respite | 1 Blood Flask + 2 Ghost Peppers | Restore 50% health + 50% energy |
| Crimson Elixir | 1 Enhanced Blood Flask + 1 Vampire Vine + 1 Ruby | Full restore (health + energy + mana) |

Stick to Enhanced Blood Flasks for 90% of situations. Crimson Elixirs are boss-fight only; the Ruby ingredient is too rare to use casually.

### Transformation & Utility

| Potion | Ingredients | Effect |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Alter Ego Elixir | 5 Glowflowers + 1 Ghost Pepper + 1 Moonstone | Change appearance for 4 hours |
| Wolf's Bane Brew | 3 Wolfsbane + 1 Vampire Vine | Temporary wolf form (combat buff) |
| Mermaid's Kiss | 2 Pearl + 3 Seaweed | Breathe underwater for 4 hours |
| Shadow Walk Tonic | 1 Shadow Silk + 5 Nightshade | Invisible to NPCs for 1 hour |
| Forget-Me-Not Potion | 5 Wolfsbane + 1 Void Essence | Reset relationship to 0 hearts |

Alter Ego Elixir is the most versatile potion. Use it to:
- Enter the Crimson Lounge without Seraphina's invitation
- Walk through human-only areas during the day (yes, you can briefly go outside during the day with this; 5 real minutes before it wears off)
- Talk to NPCs who hate vampires (Olivia, Thomas) without the initial relationship penalty

## Ingredient Farming Guide

Do not forage for ingredients. Foraging is inconsistent and time-wasting. Grow everything:

| Ingredient | Grow/Farm Location | Notes |
|------------|-------------------|-------|
| Nightshade | Farm, any season | Plant 20+ at all times. Used in everything. |
| Glowflower | Farm, spring/summer | Grows at farm edges (needs partial shade). |
| Ghost Pepper | Farm, spring | Replant every harvest. |
| Wolfsbane | Farm, fall | Most important fall crop. |
| Vampire Vine | Farm, fall | Grows on trellises (craft 2 wood + 1 iron). |
| Moonstone | Mine, levels 20-40 | Most common on level 35. |
| Void Essence | Mine, levels 60+ | Drops from Shadow Wraiths (rare enemy). |
| Pearl | Diving (Mermaid Cove) | Best farm: dive on nights with full moon (Summer 14, Fall 15).
| Ruby | Mine, levels 80+ | Extremely rare. Save for Crimson Elixirs. |

## The Cauldron: Experimental Brewing

The Potion Station has an "Experimental" tab where you can combine random ingredients. This is not random; there is a hidden recipe system. Documented discoveries:

| Combo | Result |
|-------|--------|
| Nightshade + Ghost Pepper + Moonstone | Random transformation (any form) |
| Wolfsbane + Batwing Bean + Bloodmoss | Storm Brew (summons lightning that auto-waters all crops) |
| Frostberry + Icecap Mushroom + Moonstone | Frozen Time (freezes the clock for 1 in-game hour) |
| Glowflower + Sunfruit + Pearl | Daywalker Potion (walk in sunlight for 5 minutes) |

The Daywalker Potion is the single best discovery; 5 minutes of daytime access lets you visit daytime-only NPCs and shops, which otherwise require the Alter Ego Elixir.
