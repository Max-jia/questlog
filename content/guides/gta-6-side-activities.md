GTA V had golf, tennis, yoga, and triathlons. GTA 6 is adding fishing, scuba diving, and gang raids. Here is every confirmed activity you can do when you are not on a mission.

---

### Fishing (new to GTA)

Confirmed through trailer footage. Gambit Bay is a named fishing location. The Shitzu Squalo watercraft is equipped for fishing. Expect different species, sizes, and possibly a fishing challenge system.

Rockstar brought fishing from RDR2. It was one of the most relaxing activities in that game. GTA 6 will likely expand it.

---

### Street racing

South Beach at night. The Stockyard arts district. Off-road Mud Club events. Street racing is confirmed through multiple trailer scenes. The '67 Vapid Dominator Buggy is specifically tied to Mud Club events.

---

### Gang compound raids

A new activity type. Assault gang hideouts, eliminate hostiles, escape with contraband. The PTT Youngin$ compound is named in promotional material. These are structured missions, not random world events.

---

### Scuba diving

Scuba gear is visible on the Shitzu Squalo. The Leonida Keys and extensive coastline suggest underwater exploration will be significant. Shipwrecks, reefs, and hidden treasure are expected.

---

### Classic car restoration

Collector Wyman gives you a "Special Commission" to find abandoned classic cars across Leonida and restore them. GTA's version of Forza Horizon barn finds.

---

### What returns from GTA V

- Golf
- Tennis
- Triathlons
- Strip clubs
- Hunting (confirmed through wildlife in Mount Kalaga)
- Movie theaters
