Certain biomes will kill you just by being there. Not predators. Not oxygen. The environment itself. Here is how to not die to water temperature.

---

### Heat zones

### Hydrothermal Vents (50-150m)
The water is hot enough to damage you. Constantly. You cannot stay here without Heat Tolerance.

Get the Heat Tolerance Adaptation from the Blighted Area Angel Comb. The Blighted Area is east of Old Habitat. Bloom Cankers block the entrance. Clear them with the Sonic Resonator.

Once you have Heat Tolerance, the Vents become safe. Gold and Sulfur are only here. Plan one dedicated expedition to clear out the resources.

### Hot Caves (under Tailings Village)
Same heat. Same requirement. Infinite thermal power if you build a base here.

---

### Cold zones

The deep ocean is cold. Below 200 meters, the water temperature drops. But cold does not kill you in Subnautica 2. It is uncomfortable. It is not lethal. You do not need cold protection.

If a guide tells you to craft cold-resistant gear, it is for Subnautica 1 or Below Zero. Not this game.

---

### Radiation

Radiation zones do not exist in the current Early Access build. The Aurora reactor meltdown was Subnautica 1. There is no radiation in Subnautica 2. Ignore radiation warnings in old guides.
