### Eagle Vision is not optional

Press it. Use it constantly. It marks enemies in red, objectives in green, and interactables in yellow. Hold RT on an enemy to tag them permanently through walls. If you are not using Eagle Vision, you are playing on hard mode.

---

### Whistle from cover

Bushes. Haystacks. Behind walls. Whistle. One guard will investigate. Kill them silently. Whistle again. Repeat. This clears entire patrols without raising alarms.

---

### Rooftops are your friend

Guards patrol streets. They rarely look up. Use rooftops to bypass most enemies. The parkour system is designed for this. Stay high. Stay unseen.

---

### Hide bodies

Guards who find dead bodies raise alarms. If you kill someone in the open, move the body into a bush or haystack. Or just do not kill anyone you do not need to.
