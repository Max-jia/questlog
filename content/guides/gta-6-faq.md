### Release & Platforms

**Q: When does GTA 6 release?**
A: **November 19, 2026.** Available at midnight local time per region.

**Q: What platforms is GTA 6 on?**
A: **PS5 and Xbox Series X|S only** at launch. No PS4, Xbox One, or Nintendo Switch versions.

**Q: Will GTA 6 be on PC?**
A: Yes, but **not at launch.** Expect 2027-2028 based on Rockstar's historical PC release patterns.

**Q: How much does GTA 6 cost?**
A: Standard Edition: **$79.99.** Ultimate Edition: **$99.99.**

**Q: Is there a physical disc version?**
A: Yes, but the box contains a **download code**, not a disc. You'll need to download the game.

---

### Story & Characters

**Q: Who are the main characters?**
A: **Jason Duval** (former soldier) and **Lucia Caminos** (first female protagonist in a numbered GTA). A modern Bonnie & Clyde duo.

**Q: Where is GTA 6 set?**
A: **Leonida**, a fictional version of modern-day Florida, centered on **Vice City** (Miami). Six distinct regions spanning urban, swamps, keys, mountains, and industrial zones.

**Q: Is Tommy Vercetti in GTA 6?**
A: Not confirmed as a character, but the **Vercetti Estate** exists in the game, and Tommy's Hawaiian shirt pattern is available as a weapon skin. Phil Cassidy (from GTA: Vice City) appears on in-game TV.

**Q: Can you switch between Jason and Lucia?**
A: **Yes.** Like GTA V's character switching, but more intimate with two protagonists.

---

### Map & World

**Q: How big is the GTA 6 map?**
A: Estimated **1.5-2× larger** than GTA V (~83 sq miles). Over **700 enterable interiors** vs ~150 in GTA V.

**Q: What cities are in the game?**
A: **Vice City** is the main urban hub, with four sub-locations: Port Gellhorn, Ambrosia, Grassrivers settlements, and Leonida Keys.

**Q: Is there wildlife?**
A: Yes. Confirmed: alligators, flamingos, sharks, dolphins, boars, snakes, mountain lions. Hunting is expected.

**Q: Is the whole map open from the start?**
A: Likely not. GTA games traditionally unlock regions as the story progresses.

---

### Gameplay

**Q: Is there fishing in GTA 6?**
A: **Yes.** First time in GTA history. Gambit Bay is a confirmed fishing location.

**Q: Are there heists?**
A: Almost certainly. Raul Bautista is confirmed as a veteran bank robber recruiting for "big scores."

**Q: Can you customize cars?**
A: Yes, and for the **first time, vehicle interior customization** is included.

**Q: How does the weapon system work?**
A: **RDR2-style limited loadout.** You choose weapons before missions. Weapon lockers at safehouses. Larger rifles require a sling.

**Q: Is there a first-person mode?**
A: Not officially confirmed, but expected (GTA V and RDR2 both had it).

**Q: Are there RPG elements / character stats?**
A: Not confirmed. RDR2 had health/stamina/deadeye cores. GTA VI may borrow some RPG-lite elements.

---

### Multiplayer

**Q: Is GTA 6 Online available at launch?**
A: Rockstar has described the game as a **single-player experience at launch.** GTA Online 2.0 is expected but may come post-launch, similar to GTA V in 2013.

**Q: Can you play co-op?**
A: Not in the traditional sense. Some missions feature both Jason and Lucia working together (AI-controlled partner), but **full co-op multiplayer is not confirmed for launch.**
