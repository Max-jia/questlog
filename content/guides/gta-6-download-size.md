### How Big Will GTA 6 Be? ~150-200 GB

Rockstar hasn't confirmed the exact size, but we can estimate based on their recent games:

| Game | Install Size | Map Size | Year |
|------|-------------|----------|------|
| GTA V (launch) | ~65 GB | 49 sq miles | 2013 |
| GTA V (current) | 100+ GB | 49 sq miles | 2024 |
| RDR2 | 150 GB | 75 sq miles | 2018 |
| **GTA VI (estimated)** | **150-200 GB** | **83+ sq miles** | **2026** |

GTA 6 has a map 1.5-2× larger than GTA V, 700+ interiors (vs ~150), 4K textures, and higher-fidelity assets. 150 GB is the **minimum** estimate. 200 GB is realistic.

### Pre-Load Schedule

| Date | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| **Nov 12, 2026** | Pre-load begins (digital pre-orders) |
| **Nov 19, 2026** | Launch, game unlocks at midnight local time |

With a 150-200 GB download, a 1 Gbps connection takes about 20-25 minutes. A 50 Mbps connection takes 7-8 hours. If your internet is slow, **do not wait until launch day to start downloading.**

---

### How to Free Up Space Before November

**On PS5:**
- Settings → Storage → Console Storage → Games and Apps
- Delete games you haven't played in 6+ months
- Consider an internal M.2 SSD expansion (PS5 supports up to 4TB)

**On Xbox Series X|S:**
- My Games & Apps → Sort by size
- Delete games you've finished
- Seagate Storage Expansion Card or USB external drive for cold storage

**Target:** Free up at least **200 GB** before November 12.

---

### Physical Edition, Still a Download

The physical edition of GTA 6 contains a **download code only, no disc.** You will still need to download the full 150-200 GB. Buying physical does not save you storage space or download time.
