### Collector Leviathan, The Gatekeeper

**Location:** The trench between Coral Gardens and Karakorum (east of the Graveyard). A second one patrols Metal Farms at 300m+.

**Patrol pattern:** Circles the trench on a fixed ~45-second loop. Predictable. Exploitable.

**How to cross the trench:**
1. Approach the trench entrance and **stop.**
2. Watch the Collector for one full patrol cycle (~45 seconds).
3. The moment it passes your position and begins moving away, **go.**
4. Dash through at full Tadpole speed (Scout Ray Chassis helps).
5. Do not stop in the middle of the trench. Do not turn around.

**Equipment that helps:**
- Cavitation Muffler (reduces detection range by roughly 30%)
- Scout Ray Chassis (faster than Haul Chassis)
- Impact Armor (reduces damage if caught)

**What NOT to do:** Freeze. The Collector is faster than your Tadpole. If it spots you, evasive vertical zigzagging is your only survival option. Straight-line running = death.

### Great Jaw Leviathan, The Ambush Predator

**Location:** The Hole, a deep crater in Coral Gardens (100m+).

**Behavior:** Lies motionless at the center of the crater, jaws open. When you swim close, it snaps shut, trapping you inside. You take damage until you escape or die.

**How to survive The Hole:**
- Hug the walls. The Great Jaw waits in the exact center.
- If trapped: use Sonic Resonator (or Feedback Resonator) to stun it. This forces the jaws open.
- Swim out and ascend immediately.
- Don't go back to The Hole's center. There's nothing there worth dying for.

**Is it worth visiting?** Yes, The Hole has Lithium and rare fragments around its edges. stay on the perimeter.

---

### Deepwing Brooder, Territorial Nest Guardian

**Location:** Old Habitat region (20-60m). Specifically, it guards a nesting area in the center of the ruined settlement.

**Behavior:** Fast, persistent, hard to shake if you enter its territory. It will chase you beyond its nest if provoked.

**How to handle it:**
- Avoid the center of Old Habitat unless you have a specific objective there.
- Use the ruined buildings as cover, the Deepwing can't fit through doorways.
- Dash (Biomod) helps create distance, but the Deepwing is faster. Use structures, not speed.
- If you need to scan something in its territory: have one player (in co-op) distract it while the other scans.

---

### Epicurean, Deep Zone Hunter

**Location:** Karakorum Power Plant area and Metal Farms (200m+).

**Behavior:** Aggressive free-roaming predator. High damage. Found in late-game zones where oxygen, depth, and escape options are already limited, making it disproportionately dangerous.

**How to handle it:**
- Impact Armor on the Tadpole is mandatory for these zones.
- Keep the Tadpole between you and the Epicurean, it targets the vehicle first.
- Feedback Resonator stuns it longer than the basic Sonic Resonator.
- Repair your Tadpole after every Epicurean encounter. A damaged Tadpole at 300m is death.
