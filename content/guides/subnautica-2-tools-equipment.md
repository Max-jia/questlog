## Early Game Essentials (0-2 Hours)

Craft these immediately, in this order:

| Priority | Tool | Materials | Why |
|----------|------|-----------|-----|
| 🔴 1 | **Survival Multitool** | 3× Titanium | Cannot harvest anything without it. Absolute first craft. |
| 🔴 2 | **Scanner** | 2× Ti, 2× Quartz, 1× Basic Battery | Unlocks all blueprints. Never leave base without it. |
| 🟡 3 | **Basic Fins** | 2× Rubber, 2× Fiber | Faster movement. Saves oxygen on every dive. |
| 🟡 4 | **Standard Air Tank** | 2× Ti, 1× Rubber, 2× Silver | 75 oxygen pool. Required for any dive below 10m. |
| 🟡 5 | **Air Bladder** | 2× Ti, 1× Rubber | Emergency oxygen + rapid ascent. Budget life-saver. |
| 🟢 6 | **Habitat Builder** | 2× Ti, 1× Glass, 1× Battery, 1× Copper Wire | Build your base. Mid-game essential. |

## Mid Game Tools (2-10 Hours)

| Tool | Materials | Use |
|------|-----------|-----|
| **Sonic Resonator** | 1× Battery, 2× Titanium Ingot, 2× Lead, 1× Wiring Kit | Break large resource nodes, clear Bloom Cankers, stun creatures |
| **Wakemaker** | 1× Silver, 1× Wiring Kit, 1× Grease, 1× Battery | Underwater speed boost + built-in flashlight. Replaces Flashlight entirely. |
| **Repair Tool** | 1× Titanium Ingot, 1× Wiring Kit, 1× Battery, 1× Sulfur | Fix damaged Tadpole, base modules, and systems |
| **Rebreather** | 2× Fiber Mesh, 1× System Chip | Reduces oxygen consumption at depth. Mandatory below 200m. |

### Air Tanks

| Tier | Item | Materials | Oxygen |
|------|------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Standard Air Tank | 2× Ti, 1× Rubber, 2× Silver | 75 |
| 2 | High Capacity Air Tank | 1× Standard Tank + 1× Plasteel Ingot | ~100 |
| 3 | Ultra High Capacity Air Tank | 1× High Capacity + 3× Troilite + 3× Atacamite | ~150 |

|------|------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | Basic Fins | 2× Rubber, 2× Fiber | +10% |
| 2 | Improved Fins | 1× Basic Fins + 1× Fiber Mesh + 2× Conduit Crystal | +25% |

## Tool Upgrades (Modification Station)

Built from: 2× Titanium, 2× Celestine, 2× Copper (found at Karakorum Observatory).

| Upgrade | Base Tool | Additional Materials | Effect |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|--------|
| **Bioscanner** | Scanner | 2× Enameled Glass, 3× Conduit Crystal | Scans living organisms for blueprints AND biomods |
| **Feedback Resonator** | Sonic Resonator | 2× Enameled Glass, 2× Conduit Crystal, 2× Strontium | Longer range, longer stun duration on creatures |

## Tools You Can Skip (or Delay)

| Tool | Why to Skip |
|------|------------|
| **Flashlight** | Wakemaker has a built-in flashlight. Stop using the standalone Flashlight once you craft the Wakemaker. |
| **Biosampling Kit** | Single-use. Only needed if you want specific biomods. Most players never craft it. |
| **Pathfinder Tool** | Not available in current Early Access build. |

## Pro Tips

- **Always carry a spare Battery.** Tools drain faster than you think, especially the Sonic Resonator.
- **Hotkey your tools.** Don't open inventory mid-panic. Assign tools to slots 1-5.
- **Repair your Tadpole after every expedition.** A damaged Tadpole at 300m is a death sentence.
- **Upgrade to Improved Fins early.** The 25% speed boost adds up over dozens of hours of swimming.
