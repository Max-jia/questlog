Bringing the wrong element to a boss fight is the #1 reason players wipe. Here is the complete chart. Memorize it.

---

### Type chart

| Type | Strong Against | Weak To |
|------|:---:|:---:|
| Fire | Grass, Ice | Water |
| Water | Fire | Electric |
| Electric | Water | Ground |
| Grass | Ground | Fire |
| Ground | Electric | Grass, Ice |
| Ice | Dragon | Fire |
| Dragon | Dark | Ice |
| Dark | Neutral | Dragon |

For every other combination, damage is neutral. Neutral is fine. Resisted is bad. Super effective is double damage.
