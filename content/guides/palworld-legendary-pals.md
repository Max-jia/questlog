Legendary Pals are the strongest creatures in the game. They spawn at fixed locations, at fixed levels, and they will wreck you if you show up underprepared. Here is where to find each one and how to not waste your spheres.

---

## Before you go

You need three things before hunting legendaries:
- Level 45 or higher. Level 40 is possible but you will burn through spheres.
- Legendary Spheres. Not Ultra. Not Hyper. Legendary. Bring at least 30 per boss.
- A weapon that counters their element. Fire beats ice. Ice beats dragon.

---

## Jetragon — the fastest mount in the game

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Location | -789, -321 (west of volcano) |
| Level | 50 |
| Type | Dragon |
| Weakness | Ice |

Jetragon is the fastest flying mount and the hardest legendary to catch. Bring ice weapons. Bring 50+ Legendary Spheres. Lower its health below 10%. Be patient. You will miss a lot of throws.

---

## Frostallion — the ice tank

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Location | 446, 681 (north, extremely cold) |
| Level | 48 |
| Type | Ice |
| Weakness | Fire |

Easier than Jetragon but still a serious fight. Bring fire weapons and cold-resistant armor or you will freeze to death before the fight starts. 30+ Legendary Spheres.

---

## Necromus + Paladius — the duo fight

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Location | 446, 681 (same as Frostallion, different time) |
| Level | 50 |
| Type | Dark / Neutral |
| Weakness | Dragon / Dark |

These two spawn together. You have to fight both at once. Bring a strong Pal to tank one while you focus the other. Target Necromus first — it is the more dangerous of the two.

---

## What you should NOT do

Do not show up at level 35 with Ultra Spheres. You will waste 50 of them and give up. Legendaries have low catch rates. Accept that you will burn through spheres. Accept that you might fail the first time. Come back with more spheres and a better plan.
