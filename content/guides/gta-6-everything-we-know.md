### The Protagonists

### Jason Duval
Former soldier who grew up around criminals. After the army, he worked for drug runners in the Florida Keys. First main-series protagonist with a military background, expect tactical combat abilities.

### Lucia Caminos
**First female protagonist** in a numbered GTA. Grew up in Liberty City, taught to fight by her father. Served time in Leonida Penitentiary. Puerto Rican/Latina descent.

**Together:** A modern Bonnie & Clyde story. The game focuses on their relationship and a "simple job gone wrong" that pulls them into a statewide criminal conspiracy.

### The Map: State of Leonida

Leonida is a fictional version of modern-day Florida, much larger and denser than GTA V's Los Santos.

### Six Confirmed Regions

| Region | Real Inspiration | Vibe |
|--------|-----------------|------|
| **Vice City** | Miami | Neon metropolis, beaches, downtown, Little Havana, Ocean Beach |
| **Leonida Keys** | Florida Keys | Tropical island chain, bridges, smuggling routes |
| **Grassrivers** | Everglades | Swamps, airboats, alligators, criminal hideouts |
| **Port Gellhorn** | Gulf Coast industrial | Ports, biker gangs, cheap motels |
| **Ambrosia** | Rural Florida | Small towns, factories, rail yards |
| **Mount Kalaga National Park** | Florida highlands | Mountains, hunting, off-road trails |

### Vice City Neighborhoods
Ocean Beach, South Beach, Little Havana, Little Haiti, Stockyard (Wynwood-style arts district), Venetian Islands, Port Vice City, International Airport.

### Key Gameplay has

- **Dual protagonist switching**, like GTA V, but more intimate (2 characters, not 3)
- **Living open world**, NPCs have daily routines, random events, interactive businesses
- **In-game social media**, viral videos, influencers, world events on your phone
- **RDR2-style weapon loadout**, limited carry, choose loadout before missions, weapon lockers
- **Vehicle interior customization**, new to the series
- **Fishing**, first time in GTA
- **Fencing stolen goods**, deposit at garages, sell to fences
- **Gang compound raids**, assault hideouts, escape with contraband
- **Classic car restoration**, find abandoned cars, restore them (like Forza Horizon barn finds)
- **700+ enterable interiors**, clubs, malls, pawn shops, metro stations
- **Dynamic weather**, hurricanes, storms, heatwaves affect gameplay
