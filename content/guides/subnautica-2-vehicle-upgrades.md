The Tadpole has 4 module slots and you have access to 7 modules. You cannot equip them all and some of them cost Troilite, the rarest resource in the game. Here is what to get and in what order.

---

## Upgrade priority order

### 1. Engine Efficiency ×2 (first two slots)
Each reduces energy consumption by 20%. Two of them makes your Tadpole last twice as long between charges. This is the biggest quality-of-life upgrade and the first thing you should craft.

Materials per module: 1 Titanium Ingot, 2 Glass, 1 System Chip. Cheap. Do this immediately.

### 2. Depth Module Mk.1 (third slot)
Without this, you cannot go below 200 meters. Karakorum, Metal Farms, the Observatory, everything past mid-game requires this module. Craft it before your first Karakorum expedition.

Materials: 3 Celestine, 2 Enameled Glass, 1 System Chip.

### 3. Impact Armor (fourth slot)
Reduces damage from collisions and creature bites. Karakorum has Epicureans and a second Collector Leviathan. You will get hit. Impact Armor makes those hits survivable.

Materials: 2 Enameled Glass, 2 Strontium.

---

## Modules to get later

### Cavitation Muffler
Reduces predator detection range by roughly 30%. Makes crossing the Collector Leviathan trench safer. Get this before your first trench crossing if you can afford the materials (3 Titanium, 2 Strontium). Swap it in for the crossing, then swap Impact Armor back.

### Photovoltaic Charger
Solar recharges your Tadpole in shallow water. Useful in Coral Gardens, useless in Karakorum. A nice-to-have, not a priority.

Materials: 1 Copper Ingot, 1 Strong Acid, 1 Troilite. Troilite is rare. Do not spend it on this until you have your Depth Module.

---

## Chassis: Scout Ray or Haul?

| Chassis | Best For | Materials |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Scout Ray | Exploring new biomes, predator evasion | 2 Plasteel Ingot, 1 Advanced Wiring Kit, 1 Dedicated Core, 1 Strong Acid |
| Haul | Resource farming runs, base building | 4 Titanium Ingot, 3 Strontium, 3 Enameled Glass, 1 Dedicated Core |

Scout Ray is faster and more agile. Use it when you are exploring new areas where predators are a threat. Haul adds 30 inventory slots and 2 locker anchor points. Use it when you are doing resource runs.

Swap at the Vehicle Fabricator inside your Moonpool. You are not locked into one chassis.
