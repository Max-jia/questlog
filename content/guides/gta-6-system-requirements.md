### The Short Answer

GTA 6 launches **November 19, 2026** on **PS5 and Xbox Series X|S only.** No PC. No PS4. No Xbox One. No Switch 2 (yet).

If you own both consoles, the PS5 version has an edge thanks to DualSense has. But both versions will play identically in terms of content and story.

### Why PS5 Has the Edge

**DualSense Controller.** Rockstar has confirmed haptic feedback and adaptive triggers for GTA 6 on PS5. This means:
- Trigger resistance changes per weapon (pistol vs shotgun feel different)
- Road surface texture transmitted through haptics while driving
- Environmental effects (rain, explosions) felt through the controller

**3D Audio.** PS5's Tempest 3D audio with compatible headsets creates positional sound, hear helicopters above you, footsteps behind you, and gunfire directionally.

---

### Why Xbox Has Advantages

**Quick Resume.** Switch between GTA 6 and other games instantly. Jump from a mission straight back into your paused session without loading.

**Xbox Game Pass Potential.** While GTA 6 is not confirmed for Game Pass at launch, Microsoft's acquisition strategy and Rockstar's history with Game Pass (GTA V was available) make it likely to arrive eventually.

**Series S Budget Option.** At $299, the Series S is the cheapest way to play GTA 6 at launch. You sacrifice resolution and performance mode, but the game is the same.

---

### Storage Warning: Clear Space Now

Rockstar games are massive. GTA V with updates is over 100 GB. Red Dead Redemption 2 is 150 GB. GTA 6, with its larger map, 700+ interiors, and higher-fidelity assets, is expected to require **150-200 GB.**

**Prepare now:**
- Delete games you haven't played in 6 months
- Consider an NVMe SSD expansion card (Xbox) or M.2 SSD upgrade (PS5)
- Pre-load begins November 12, that's when you'll need the space

### PC Version, When?

Rockstar's PC track record:

| Game | Console Launch | PC Launch | Gap |
|------|---------------|-----------|-----|
| GTA V | Sep 2013 | Apr 2015 | 19 months |
| RDR2 | Oct 2018 | Nov 2019 | 13 months |
| **GTA VI (predicted)** | **Nov 2026** | **2027-2028** | **12-24 months** |

Expect GTA 6 on PC sometime between late 2027 and early 2028. The PC version will likely feature higher framerates, ultrawide support, and modding capabilities, but you'll be waiting at least a year.

### Recommended Setup for Launch Day

**On Any Console:**
- 4K TV/monitor with HDR (the game is visually stunning)
- SSD with at least 200 GB free
- Wired internet for day-one patch download
- Headset for 3D audio / voice chat

**If You're Buying a Console for GTA 6:**
- **Best overall:** PS5 (DualSense is worth it)
- **Budget pick:** Xbox Series S ($299)
- **If you care about Game Pass:** Xbox Series X
