## You Just Crashed on Proteus. Now What?

You crashed on an alien ocean planet. No food. No tools. No idea what's down there. Follow these steps, in this exact order, and you'll be running a self-sufficient underwater base within hours.

---

### Craft the Multitool First, You Can't Do Anything Without It

**Materials:** 3× Titanium

Grab 3× Titanium from the seabed around your Lifepod. Craft this **immediately**, you can't harvest anything without it.

---

### Craft the Scanner Second, It Unlocks Every Blueprint

**Materials:** 2× Titanium, 2× Quartz, 1× Basic Battery

Quartz: on the seabed and in caves. Basic Battery: 1× Copper + 1× Acidic Raion Pouch.

**Why this matters:** No scanner = no blueprints = no progress. Every tool, base part, and vehicle requires scanning first.

---

### Find the Digestion Angel Comb (NE, 160m), Or You'll Starve

Head **northeast** from your Lifepod approximately 160 meters. Look for a glowing purple plant called an **Angel Comb**. Interact with it to unlock the **Digestion** adaptation.

**Without Digestion, you cannot safely eat alien food.** You'll get sick from eating local fish. This is the #1 mistake new players make, they starve because they don't know about this adaptation.

**Bonus:** While heading northeast, grab **Water Slugs** from the seabed. One Water Slug = one bottle of water in the Fabricator.

---

### Equip Fins, Air Tank, and Air Bladder Before Exploring Deeper

Before exploring further, craft these:

| Item | Materials | Why You Need It |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| **Basic Fins** | 2× Rubber, 2× Fiber | Faster swimming speed. major change. |
| **Standard Air Tank** | 2× Titanium, 1× Rubber, 2× Silver | Extends oxygen to 75. Non-negotiable. |
| **Air Bladder** | 2× Titanium, 1× Rubber | Emergency oxygen + rapid ascent. Saves lives. |

**Where to find Silver:** The Blighted Area and Old Habitat region (20-60m depth, explore east/southeast of Lifepod).

**Where to find Rubber:** Lucifer Rotsac plants in the Shallows and Old Habitat area.

---

### Activate the Welcome Center (SE, 100m) for Biomods

The Welcome Center is located **southeast** of your Lifepod, approximately 100 meters away. It's a large ruined structure.

1. Enter the Welcome Center.
2. Find the power outlet on the wall.
3. Insert a **Basic Battery**.
4. The Biolab inside activates, you can now research **Biomods**.

**Recommended first Biomods:**
- **Active: Dash**, short speed burst, great for escaping predators
- **Passive: Sea Skimmer**, faster movement near the water surface

---

### Build a Real Base, The Lifepod Fabricator Is Limited

Scan **2 Habitat Builder fragments** at the Welcome Center (and another at Camp One in Jelly Barrens, to the northeast). Once unlocked:

1. Craft the **Habitat Builder**: 2× Titanium, 1× Glass, 1× Basic Battery, 1× Copper Wire.
2. Find a flat area near resources (Shallows or Coral Fields).
3. Build in this order: **Room** (5× Titanium), **Hatch** (1× Ti + 1× Quartz), **Solar Panel**, **Fabricator** (in-base).

**Why build a base:** The Lifepod Fabricator has limitations. A base Fabricator unlocks the full crafting tree. Plus, you get storage lockers, battery chargers, and actual room to organize.

---

### Follow the Black Boxes, They're Your Story Guide

Your AI companion NOA will direct you to Black Boxes, crash sites of other colonists. These are your primary story and progression markers.

**Early priority Black Boxes:** Anita, Chap, Wander, Quaker

Each Black Box contains:
- Story lore
- Critical blueprints
- Directions to the next objective

## Common Beginner Mistakes

- **Not crafting a Scanner early.** You can't unlock blueprints without scanning.
- **Eating fish before getting Digestion.** You'll get sick and lose health.
- **Ignoring base building.** You need a real base with storage and a Fabricator.
- **Swimming too deep without air upgrades.** Oxygen runs out fast. Respect depth.
- **Not reading NOA's dialogue.** She tells you exactly where to go next.
