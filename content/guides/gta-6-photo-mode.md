RDR2's photo mode was one of the most beloved features in any Rockstar game. GTA 6 will have one too. Here is what we know and what to expect.

---

### Confirmed

Rockstar job listings for GTA 6 included roles specifically mentioning a photo mode. The trailers show in-game photography: NPCs taking photos, social media posts with filters, and a smartphone camera interface.

A photo mode in the world's most detailed open-world city is not speculation. It is happening.

---

### What to expect (based on RDR2)

RDR2's photo mode included:
- Free camera movement within a radius of the player
- Depth of field controls
- Exposure and contrast sliders
- Time-of-day and weather toggles
- Filter presets
- Social club sharing

GTA 6 will likely match or exceed all of these. The in-game social media system (confirmed) suggests photos will be shareable within the game world.
