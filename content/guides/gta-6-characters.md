### The Protagonists

### Jason Duval
**Role:** Former soldier turned criminal

- First main-series GTA protagonist with a military background
- Grew up around criminals in the Florida Keys
- After the army, worked for drug runners, knows the waterways and smuggling routes
- His safehouse is at Paradise Garage (Watson Bay)
- Access to: Dinka Enduro motorcycle, Crest kayak, off-road vehicles
- Special skills likely include combat tactics, weapon handling, and off-road driving

### Lucia Caminos
**Role:** Street-smart survivor

- **First female protagonist** in a numbered GTA title
- Grew up in Liberty City, taught to fight by her father
- Puerto Rican / Latina descent
- Served time in Leonida Penitentiary (shown in Trailer 1)
- The game opens with her release from prison
- Special skills likely include stealth, social manipulation, and close-quarters combat

### Their Dynamic

Rockstar describes their story as: *"A simple job goes wrong, pulling the duo into a state-wide criminal conspiracy. They must rely on each other more than ever."*

The game focuses on an intimate Bonnie & Clyde relationship, more personal than GTA V's three-protagonist sprawl. You can switch between Jason and Lucia at any time during free roam. Some missions feature them working together (co-op style), while others are solo.

### Supporting Characters

### Cal Hampton
Jason's friend. A conspiracy theorist who monitors Coast Guard comms, providing intelligence on law enforcement movements and waterway patrols. Likely your "guy in the chair" for smuggling missions.

### Boobie Ike
Vice City legend and entertainment mogul. Owns:
- A strip club (club management missions likely)
- Real estate holdings
- Only Raw Records recording studio
- Various legitimate (and illegitimate) businesses

He's your connection to the Vice City nightlife scene and entertainment industry.

### Dre'Quan Priest
Music producer behind Only Raw Records. Connected to the Vice City hip-hop scene. Likely involved in music industry missions and celebrity-related storylines.

### Real Dimez (Bae-Luxe & Roxy)
Female rap duo and social media influencers. Their presence confirms the in-game social media system will be a major feature, expect missions involving viral stunts, influencer culture, and internet fame.

### Raul Bautista
Veteran bank robber recruiting for big scores. He's your heist connection, the Lester Crest of GTA VI. If you want to rob banks, he's the one to talk to.

### Brian Heder
Old-school drug runner and Jason's landlord. Connects Jason to the Florida drug trade. A throwback to the 1980s Vice City era, an aging criminal in a modern world.

### Wyman
Eccentric car collector. Tied to the Classic Car Collection commission (Ultimate Edition activity). His missions involve finding and restoring abandoned classic cars across Leonida.
