## Trailer 1, December 5, 2023

Released without warning. Broke the YouTube record for most views in 24 hours: **90.4 million**. Set to Tom Petty's "Love Is a Long Road."

### Key Reveals

- **Lucia in prison.** The trailer opens with Lucia in Leonida Penitentiary, wearing an orange jumpsuit. An ankle monitor is later visible, she's on parole.
- **Modern Vice City.** Neon-soaked skyline, Ocean Beach, South Beach nightlife, Vice City International Airport.
- **Social media integration.** Multiple shots of in-game TikTok-style videos, NPCs filming themselves doing stunts, dancing, and committing crimes.
- **Wildlife.** Alligators in swimming pools and swamps. Flamingos. A shark. This is Florida, wildlife is everywhere.
- **The Bonnie & Clyde dynamic.** Jason and Lucia rob a diner together. They argue in a motel room. They trust each other. The relationship is the story.

### Easter Eggs Spotted

- Reference to the "Leonida Man" (Florida Man) news headlines.
- A Thrillbilly Mud Club off-road event visible in a social media clip.
- Ocean Beach hotel names match real Miami Art Deco hotels.

## Trailer 2, May 6, 2025

Nearly 18 months after the first trailer. Confirmed the game's massive scope and introduced the six Leonida regions.

### Key Reveals

- **All six regions named and shown:** Vice City, Leonida Keys, Grassrivers, Port Gellhorn, Ambrosia, Mount Kalaga National Park.
- **Supporting cast introduced:** Cal Hampton, Boobie Ike, Dre'Quan Priest, Real Dimez, Raul Bautista, Brian Heder.
- **Gameplay glimpses:** Jason piloting an airboat through Grassrivers swamps. Lucia driving a sports car through Vice City at night. Both characters in a shootout at Port Gellhorn docks.
- **Vehicles:** Grotti Cheetah, Vapid Dominator Buggy, Shitzu Squalo watercraft, Dinka Enduro motorcycle shown.
- **Fishing confirmed.** Quick shot of a character casting a line at Gambit Bay.

### New Map Details

| Region | Trailer Shot |
|--------|-------------|
| **Vice City** | Downtown skyline, Ocean Beach boardwalk, Little Havana street scene |
| **Leonida Keys** | Bridge connecting islands, boats, crystal-clear water |
| **Grassrivers** | Airboat through sawgrass, alligator submerging, swamp shack |
| **Port Gellhorn** | Container docks at night, shootout, industrial cranes |
| **Ambrosia** | Small-town main street, rail yard, biker gang |
| **Mount Kalaga** | Forest trail, hunting cabin, mountain overlook |

## Trailer 3, Expected June 2026

Rockstar has not officially announced Trailer 3, but industry insiders expect it to drop alongside pre-order information. The pre-order launch (June 25, 2026) was accompanied by 63 new 4K screenshots, which effectively served as a "silent trailer."

### What We Expect to See

- First extended gameplay footage (beyond quick cuts)
- Vehicle customization system in detail
- More GTA Online 2.0 information
- Confirmed PC release window
- Potential celebrity cameos and radio station reveals

---

## What the Trailers Don't Show (Yet)

- GTA Online 2.0, completely absent from all promotional material so far
- Full mission gameplay, only short cutscene clips
- Character skill trees or RPG elements
- Map UI or navigation system
- First-person mode confirmation
