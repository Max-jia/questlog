Moonlight Peaks has about two dozen romanceable NPCs spanning werewolves, witches, mermaids, vampires, and humans. Each has unique gift preferences, a birthday, and heart events that unlock at specific heart levels. Here is every confirmed romance candidate with their gift preferences.

---

## Werewolves

### Fenris (Male)

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| Birthday | Spring 14 |
| Location (10PM-2AM) | The Howling Wolf Tavern |
| **Loves** | Rare Meat (hunt deer), Silver Fang Necklace (crafted) |
| Likes | Any meat dish, Wolfsbane flowers |
| **Hates** | Silver Bars (it burns him — literally, he takes damage) |

**Heart events**: The Level 5 event involves a full-moon hunt together. The Level 8 event reveals his backstory as a former Templar hunter. Bring a healing potion to the Level 5 event — there is a combat sequence.

### Luna Grey (Female)

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| Birthday | Fall 7 |
| Location (8PM-midnight) | Luna's Potion Shop |
| **Loves** | Moonflower Bouquet (crafted), Starlight Elixir |
| Likes | Any potion ingredient, Glowflower |
| **Hates** | Garlic (vampire-friendly NPCs are offended by it) |

**Heart events**: Luna's events are tied to the Witch Coven questline. You cannot progress past 6 hearts without completing Family #3's main story arc first. She is the easiest witch to romance because she works at a public shop (always accessible).

## Vampires (Fellow Undead)

### Seraphina Night (Female)

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| Birthday | Fall 31 (Halloween — 5x bonus!) |
| Location (10PM-2AM) | Crimson Lounge (VIP vampire club) |

## Marriage Requirements

1. 10 hearts with the NPC.
2. See all 4 heart events (levels 3, 5, 8, and 10).
3. Craft a Moonstone Ring (1 Moonstone + 2 Silver Bars).
4. Propose during their active hours at their usual location.
5. Wedding happens automatically 3 nights later.

**Divorce is available** — offer a Forget-Me-Not Potion (crafted from 5 Wolfsbane + 1 Void Essence) to reset the relationship to 0 hearts. This also erases all heart event memories, so the NPC acts as if they never met you.
