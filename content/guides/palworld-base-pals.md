You need different Pals for your base than your party. Combat Pals fight. Base Pals work. Mix them up and nobody does their job right. Here are the best Pals for every job.

---

### Kindling (cooking and smelting)

| Pal | Level | Where |
|-----|:---:|-------|
| Foxparks | 1 | Starting area |
| Incineram | 3 | Windswept Island (Syndicate capture) |
| Jormuntide Ignis | 4 | Breed Jormuntide + Blazehowl |

Put a Kindling Pal in every base. Without one, you do everything by hand.

### Minimize your workforce

The fewer Pals you need, the easier the base is to manage. Incineram covers Mining AND Kindling. Breed one and you have two jobs covered with one Pal slot. Anubis has Mining and Handiwork. Every Pal that covers two jobs saves you a slot.
