### Board everything

Every ship you board gives you full resources. Sinking gives you half. Boarding also repairs your ship and refills your crew. Board everything you can.

---

### Kenway's Fleet

After a story mission, you unlock Kenway's Fleet. Send captured ships on trade routes. They generate reales while you play. Check the fleet after every session. Collect the money. It adds up.

---

### Treasure maps

22 treasure maps across the Caribbean. Each one leads to buried treasure. The payout is worth it. Buy maps from vendors or find them on corpses.

---

### Assassination and naval contracts

Easy money. Pick them up from taverns and harbors. Check the difficulty before accepting. Do the easy ones. Skip the hard ones until you have a better ship.

---

### What NOT to spend money on

- Weapons early. The starting swords are fine until mid-game.
- Outfits. Purely cosmetic. Zero gameplay benefit.
- Ship cosmetics. Same. Upgrade the hull before you paint it.

Invest every real in the Jackdaw. A stronger ship earns more money. It pays for itself.
