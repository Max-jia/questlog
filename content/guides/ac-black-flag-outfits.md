### Mayan Armor — the only one that matters

Collect all Mayan Stelae scattered across the Caribbean. Each one requires a puzzle — align symbols with landmarks. Once you have them all, the Mayan Armor unlocks.

Effect: deflects bullets. This is the single best outfit in the game. Get it as early as possible.

---

### Templar Armor

Complete all Templar Hunt missions. Reduces melee damage. Less useful than Mayan Armor but worth getting for the damage reduction in close combat.

---

### Cosmetic outfits

The rest are cosmetic. Hunter Outfit. Pirate Captain. Edward's default robes. They look different but play the same. Do not grind for outfits unless you care about style.
