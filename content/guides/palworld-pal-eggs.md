Eggs are free Pals. You find them on the ground. You put them in an incubator. They hatch into something you did not have to catch. Here is every egg type and where to look.

---

### Egg types and where to find them

| Egg Type | Biome | Rare Pal Potential |
|----------|-------|:---:|
| Common | Everywhere | Low |
| Scorching | Volcano, desert | Fire Pals |
| Damp | Lakes, rivers, beaches | Water Pals |
| Frozen | Northern snow area | Ice Pals |
| Dark | Night spawns, caves | Dark Pals |
| Dragon | Volcano, high areas | Dragon Pals |
| Electric | Thunderstorms, desert | Electric Pals |
| Verdant | Forests, grasslands | Grass Pals |

---

### Egg sizes

| Size | Rarity | Incubation Time |
|------|:---:|:---:|
| Regular | Common | Short |
| Large | Uncommon | Medium |
| Huge | Rare | Long |

Huge eggs are the ones you want. They contain the rarest Pals. If you see a Huge egg, drop everything and grab it.

---

### Building the incubator

Unlock at level 7. Materials: 10 Paldium Fragment, 5 Cloth, 2 Stone, 1 Ancient Civilization Part.

Build it indoors. Temperature affects incubation speed. A campfire near the incubator speeds up Scorching eggs. An air conditioner works for all types.

---

### Hatching tips

Check eggs as you explore. They glow slightly at night. The ground spawns are fixed but respawn slowly.

If you find a Huge Dragon egg, you might get a Jetragon without fighting it. This is the easiest way to get legendary Pals without getting killed.
