## Resource Farming Tips

- **Titanium** is the backbone of everything. Always pick up scrap metal. Build a locker for Titanium.
- **Gold and Sulfur are location-locked.** You must go to Hydrothermal Vents. There is no other source. Plan expeditions.
- **Troilite respawns slowly.** The Metal Farms have limited nodes. Don't waste Troilite on non-essential recipes.
- **Use the Processor for ingots.** It uses fewer materials than the Fabricator (e.g., 3× Titanium vs 5× for an Ingot).
- **Grow plants in your base.** Lucifer Rotsac and edible flora in growbeds save endless farming trips.
