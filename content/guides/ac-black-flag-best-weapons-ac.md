### Priority order

1. **Pistol Swords.** Unlock these first. They let you shoot and slash without switching. This saves you from dying during weapon swaps in the middle of a fight.

2. **Golden Flintlocks.** Best pistols in the game. Four shots. Reload speed is manageable. Worth the reales.

3. **Upgrade the Hidden Blades.** More assassination damage. You assassinate dozens of guards. Every upgrade pays for itself.

---

### Weapon types

| Type | Best For |
|------|---------|
| Swords | All-purpose melee. Use these most. |
| Pistols | Burst damage. Fire at close range. |
| Hidden Blades | Stealth kills. Upgrade early. |
| Rope Darts | Pulling enemies and hanging them. Situational. |

---

### What to skip

- Heavy weapons. Too slow. You get hit during the windup.
- Blowpipe. Fun but useless in combat.
- Smoke bombs. Situational. Not worth the inventory slot.
